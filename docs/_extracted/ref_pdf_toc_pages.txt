6 Learnaira(Interactive E-learning platform powered by AI )
ABSTRACT
Our project encompasses an advanced electronic learning platform
meticulously designed to elevate the educational experience. Within this
innovative ecosystem, a seamless registration system and an intuitive
enrollment process give students access to a rich range of courses, all
expertly led by certified teachers. Each course listing is akin to a treasure
trove of knowledge, brimming with comprehensive instructor profiles,
course lengths, and invaluable peer reviews.
This educational haven focuses on interactive learning and fosters a
dynamic and collaborative environment through its group chat function. It
also facilitates course evaluations and provides a platform for constructive
feedback. It's important to highlight that the project integrates advanced
artificial intelligence models, including question generation and answering,
thus enhancing the depth and quality of the learning experience. These
artificial intelligence models also contribute to creating a safe and
respectful online educational space through an AI model designed to
prevent the use of inappropriate language.
Furthermore, our project offers a unique dimension where educators play
a pivotal role in enriching the learning journey. They supplement their
courses with educational videos, enhancing comprehension of the content.
Additionally, all essential PDF materials are thoughtfully provided for
comprehensive study throughout the course's duration.
